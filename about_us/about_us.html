About us

Know about us


Ruhe Hotels & Resorts is a modern, upscale hospitality company that is passionate about ‘making moments’, recognising that small gestures make a big difference to our guests, our owners and our people. We do ordinary things in an extraordinary way – a philosophy that has defined our brand’s success from the very start.


A global firm with Swiss roots and a restaurant and hospitality heritage that dates back to the 1990s, our approach to service has remained consistent; warm, intuitive, personal. We also embrace innovation to meet ever-changing guest needs and continue to celebrate our rich culinary legacy, setting trends but never compromising on quality and authenticity.
